To convert one of the tables below to a graph, open up console (F12) and type:
Agile.createGraph({dataType:'table',identifier:'#affluent_black_communities'}) or, click:
"Create Graph" in the left sidebar.

To import a csv to convert to a table and graph, open up console and type:
Agile.createGraph({dataType:'csv',identifier:'/assets/sample.csv'}) or click:
"New Dataset in the left sidebar."
